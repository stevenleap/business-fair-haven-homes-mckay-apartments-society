About Fair Haven

Fair Haven Homes McKay Apartments Society operates a low‑income senior housing facility in Vancouver, BC. Our building provides a quiet, dignified home for seniors, with common areas that encourage community and connection.

Since opening, we have focused on creating a safe, welcoming environment where residents feel respected and supported. The building is purpose‑built for senior comfort and safety, located in a quiet Vancouver neighbourhood.
